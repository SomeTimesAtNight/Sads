About the Game

Shadows of the Past is a thrilling survival game where players must navigate a post-apocalyptic world filled with challenges and deadly monsters.

  • Search for and gather essential resources.
  • Manage health, stamina, and noise levels to evade enemies.
  • Experience multiple paths and endings based on your decisions.

Game Mechanics

Discover unique features that set Shadows of the Past apart:

  • Stealth: Use the environment to sneak past enemies and stay silent.
  • Dynamic AI: Enemie adapt to your behavior, making each encounter unique.
